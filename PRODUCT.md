# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Mall parking staff, merchant staff, and administrators use ParkFlow Mall during live gate, parking, payment, reservation, and exception-handling workflows. Customers use the public ticket and reservation pages on a phone.

## Product Purpose

ParkFlow Mall coordinates a smart mall parking journey while preserving clear human approval points for gate entry, payment, and exit. The interface must help an operator act quickly and let a customer understand the status of their parking visit.

## Brand Personality

Calm, dependable, and operationally precise. It should feel suitable for a graduation-project demonstration while remaining credible for a real mall control room.

## Anti-references

Avoid toy-like controls, decorative gradients, visual noise, glassmorphism, and dashboard ornament that obscures operational state. Do not make a dense staff tool feel like a marketing landing page.

## Design Principles

- Make the current operational state immediately legible.
- Keep customer and operator contexts clearly separated.
- Use familiar controls so staff can act without interpretation.
- Keep security and human-confirmation boundaries visible in the interface.
- Preserve behavior while making the demo flow easy to navigate.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use high-contrast text, labels for every form control, keyboard-visible focus, status text in addition to color, responsive tables, and reduced-motion support. Customer ticket pages must remain comfortable on mobile.
